# Business DNA (Mission Control Master Context)

## 1. Core Identity
- **Business Name**: GravityClaw Automation Agency
- **Target Audience**: Local SMBs (Plumbers, Dentists, Lawyers, Real Estate)
- **Primary Offer**: End-to-end AI workflow automations and digital employees for $300-$500/month subscriptions.

## 2. Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional, authoritative, yet approachable and cutting-edge.
- **Key Phrasing**: We "deploy agents," "automate overhead," and "build scalable systems."
- **Brand Colors**: 
  - Primary: Deep Purple (`#a855f7`)
  - Secondary: Hot Pink (`#ec4899`)

## 3. Financial Targets & Pricing Mode
- **Tier 1 (Starter)**: $197/mo - Basic Google Review Automation + Lead Magnet CRM
- **Tier 2 (Pro)**: $497/mo - AI Appointment Setter + Social Media Engine
- **Tier 3 (Enterprise)**: $997/mo - Full Voice Agent Support + Custom Dashboards

## 4. The Top 3 Problems We Solve
1. SMBs wasting hours on manual administrative tasks (invoicing, scheduling).
2. Losing leads because nobody answers the phone or responds to DMs quickly enough.
3. Inconsistent client follow-ups leading to poor Google reviews.
