# Mission Control: Task List Overview

The Task List is the central hub for tracking work in Mission Control. Its behavior adapts intelligently depending on whether you are a Platform Admin or a Client User.

## For Client Users
When a Client logs into their dashboard, the Task List provides a focused, streamlined view of only their company's tasks.
* **Task Visibility:** Clients only see tasks assigned to their company.
* **Filtering:** The "Assignee" filter automatically updates to show only the client's internal team members and the platform "Admin".
* **Creating Tasks:** Clients can create new tasks (like bug reports, feature requests, or content approvals) and assign them directly to the platform "Admin" for review.
* **Views:** Clients can toggle between a visual "Kanban" board or a traditional "List" view to see priorities and due dates clearly.

## For Platform Admins
When a Platform Admin views the Task List, they get a master control center for all operations across the entire agency.
* **Context Switching:** Admins can quickly toggle between "Platform Tasks" (internal work) and "Client Tasks" using the top navigation filters.
* **AI Agent Assignment:** The Platform assignee filter includes the entire roster of specialized AI Agents, such as Jarvis, AntiGravity, the Content Agent, and the QA Agent. 
* **Kanban Mastery:** Admins can drag and drop tasks across columns (Backlog, To Do, In Progress, Review, Done) to easily manage the flow of work across the team.
* **Detailed Editing:** Admins can open any task and comprehensively edit its details, re-assign it to a specific client company, or hand it off to an automated AI Agent for background execution.
